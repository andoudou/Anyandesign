ONLINE SHOPPING EXPERIENCE 在线商城体验设计


In this project, we choose website for computer as the platform to design pages and interaction of online shopping website. We focuse on step of comparing different products in the whole process of choosing and buying lamps. And according to showing the virtual scene of lamps to let customers observe lamps better, which use the advantage of physical store to make up the shortage of online shopping experience.
